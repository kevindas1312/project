This project is an action-adventure role-playing game (RPG) set in a fictional world known as Ravenshire. 

The game follows a young and ambitious swordfighter who aspires to become the greatest warrior of all time. 

To achieve this goal, the player must obtain a legendary weapon known as The Bloodpetal.

In order to acquire the sword, the player must progress through three main levels, each presenting a distinct challenge and boss encounter. 

Every level features a guardian that protects a unique weapon required to advance to the next stage. 

In the first level, the player must defeat The Keeper of Frostmere in the North of Greystone to obtain The Crimson Blade. 

The second level introduces The Warden of Blackhollow, located in the Northern reaches of Ashvale, who guards The Obsidian Edge. 

The final level concludes with a battle against The Sentinel of Ironreach in the Northern Highlands of Valemont, the undefeated guardian of The Bloodpetal itself.

The game combines progressive, combat-based challenges with a simple narrative structure. 

At the beginning of the game, players will also be able to choose a default sword, allowing for a degree of customization and variation in gameplay style.
